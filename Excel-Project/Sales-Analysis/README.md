
# Sales, Product, and Customer Analysis

## Objective
Analyze sales performance, product trends, and customer behavior to identify key revenue drivers and actionable business insights.

## Tools Used
Excel (Power Query, Power Pivot, Pivot Tables, Dashboards, Recorded Macros)

## Key Analysis
- Performed time-based analysis of sales, cost, profit, and margin to evaluate overall business performance across years, months, and quarters.
- Analyzed product-level performance to identify top-performing products, product categories, and pricing segments contributing to profit.
- Conducted customer analysis to assess profit concentration among top customers, customer demographics, and customer segments.
- Evaluated geographic performance to understand country-level contributions to total profit.
- Compared weekday versus weekend performance to assess transaction and profit patterns.

## Key Insights
The insights below are based on overall performance with no filters applied and represent high-level business trends.

- Total revenue amounted to approximately $307M, with a profit margin of 41.1%, indicating strong overall profitability.
- Profit peaked in specific years, with the highlighted year contributing over 67% of  profit compared to other periods.
- A small subset of products drives a disproportionate share of profit: the top 5 products account for roughly 25% of total profit.
- High-priced products (above $150) accounted for  over 95% of total profit, while lower-priced products contributed marginally.
- Customer profit is relatively concentrated, with the top 5 customers contributing a small share relative to the broader customer base.
- Customers aged 50+ contribute the highest share of profit, accounting for over 40% of total profit.
- Weekday transactions generate the majority of profit (over 70%), outperforming weekend sales.
- Geographically, America and Australia together contribute more than 60% of total profit, making them key strategic markets.

The dashboard allows dynamic filtering by time, country, product, and customer attributes to support deeper exploratory analysis.
